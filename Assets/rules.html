Rules

You will be given 6 different tasks you have to complete over the duration of the game

If someone completes one of your tasks, click on the task and then click on the player that completed the task

Some tasks are global tasks that everyone will be given, which will be shown by the task having 'Global: ' before it

Be careful when trying to complete a task, as the more times you fail to complete it, the harder it will get to successfully complete it
